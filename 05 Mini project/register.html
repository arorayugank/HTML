Welcome to Facebook

Facebook, founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004, is a social media platform that connects billions of users worldwide. It allows users to create profiles, share updates, photos, and videos, and engage with friends and family through comments and reactions. Over the years, Facebook has expanded its services to include messaging, events, marketplace, and more. Despite facing criticism over privacy concerns, misinformation, and its impact on society, Facebook remains one of the most influential platforms globally, shaping communication, business, and culture. Its acquisitions of Instagram and WhatsApp further solidify its dominance in the social media landscape.
